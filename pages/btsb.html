Binoy The Science Boy

A parody of the hit TV show Bill Nye the Science Guy that I directed, filmed, and edited for my AP Psychology class. It helps AP Psychology students go through and review the different parts of the brain and each one's functions.

Learn More

I'm especially proud of this video because it was, at the time, the most ambitious video project I had done. At a whopping 11 minutes, it covers all the major parts of the brain in an (arguably) interesting and attention-retaining manner. The intro alone took me weeks to edit due to the many moving images to recreate. I am also proud of how I matched the classic Bill Nye style with puns, quick cuts to static, and corny jokes.
